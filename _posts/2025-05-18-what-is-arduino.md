# What is Arduino, What Does It Do?

When I was just stepping into the world of technology, I wanted to do something about electronics, but I didn't know where to start.

Arduino is actually an electronic card that you can think of as a small computer and you can load programs into it. But unlike a computer, it allows you to control the lights, motors, sensors around you. In other words, you can write commands like “turn on this light” or “start this motor” to Arduino and it does what it does.

For example, at first I started with just turning on an LED, it was very simple but very enjoyable. Then I realized that there is no limit to what I can do with Arduino. I can make automatic light systems at home, I can even design robots.

The best thing about Arduino is that it is very easy to program and very cheap. In addition, thousands of people share about this subject on the internet. That's why I always found help whenever I got stuck.

In short, if you want to enter the world of electronics and coding, Arduino is for you. With a simple hardware, an easy-to-learn programming language and plenty of resources, you can build the project of your dreams.

If you're like me and thinking “where should I start?”, I highly recommend Arduino. Take a small step and start doing big things!
