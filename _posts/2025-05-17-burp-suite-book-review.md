This week I read the book **“A Complete Guide to Burp Suite”**.  
I have never seen a book that explains Burp Suite in such detail.  
The book helped me not only to use the tool, but also to understand **web application security** in depth.

---

### 📚 Structure of the Book

Each chapter of the book makes it easy to use the **Burp Suite tool** and learn about **web application security**.  
It starts by explaining the **basics** of Burp Suite in plain language, then moves on to more **complex features**.  
While I learned the basics, I also realized how to use the **advanced features** of Burp Suite.  
The transition between chapters was very natural, so I was always curious about the next step.

---

### 🧠 Narration and Visuals

The book reinforces the information by using a lot of **visuals** in the narration.  
These visuals are very helpful alongside the written narration and make it easier for me to understand complex concepts.  
The visuals show me how to use each feature step by step.  
I really enjoyed reading it because it was presented in a format that I could immediately put into practice what I had learned.

---

### 🔌 Features and Add-ons

In the **plugins** section of the book, the recommended **extensions for Burp Suite** and how to use them are explained in detail.  
Learning how to discover and use these plugins, which can further extend the capabilities of Burp Suite, is a real game-changer.

---

📌 This was my **first book note** in the field of cybersecurity. I’ll keep reading and sharing what I learn here. 🚀
